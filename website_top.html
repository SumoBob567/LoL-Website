Best Top Lane Champions

The best top lane champions are as follows:


  • Karma

    • Plenty of sustain

    • Crowd Control

    • High Win Rate

    • Low Skill Floor and High Skill Cap

  • Janna (with smite)

    • Can roam for kills

    • Objective bounties for your team

    • Very fun to play

    • May make your opponent rage quit

  • Darius

    • High kill pressure

    • High Sustain

    • Few counters

    • Powerful in team fights.
